# Better Than Interns – Product Roadmap (Phase 1–2)

This roadmap focuses on delivering the consulting automation suite (Phase 1) and preparing for expansion into AI governance (early Phase 2).

---

## High-Level Phases

1. **Foundation & MVP (Weeks 1–6)**
2. **Opportunity & Tool Intelligence (Weeks 7–12)**
3. **Blueprint & Export Experience (Weeks 13–16)**
4. **Stabilization, Feedback, and Light Governance Prep (Weeks 17–24)**

---

## Phase 1 – Foundation & MVP (Weeks 1–6)

- Auth & workspaces.
- Projects & processes CRUD.
- Conversational Process Assistant MVP.
- Workflow graph visualization.

---

## Phase 2 – Opportunity & Tool Intelligence (Weeks 7–12)

- AI Opportunity Scanner:
  - Scoring logic.
  - ROI estimation.
  - Opportunity storage & UI.
- Tool Matching Engine:
  - Tool catalog & metadata.
  - Matching API & UI.

---

## Phase 3 – Blueprint & Export (Weeks 13–16)

- Blueprint data model.
- Blueprint generation using LLM.
- Blueprint view UI.
- Export (Markdown first, PDF optionally).

---

## Phase 4 – Stabilization & Governance Prep (Weeks 17–24)

- Prompt refinement.
- Error handling & performance.
- Early governance fields on steps/opportunities:
  - Data sensitivity.
  - Decision impact.
- Prepare groundwork for:
  - Policy generation.
  - Risk scoring.
  - Compliance mapping.

This roadmap is intentionally compact and should be used alongside `prd.md` for sprint and ticket planning.
