# Better Than Interns – Brand & Identity Guide

## 1. Brand Overview
**Better Than Interns** is a humorous, self-aware, and highly competent AI consulting + SaaS brand.  
The company simplifies business process mapping, identifies automation opportunities, and provides AI-driven insights—while embracing a witty personality that stands out in a crowded market.

---

## 2. Core Brand Idea
We replace the unreliable, overwhelmed, spreadsheet-wielding intern stereotype with an automated, intelligent system that actually knows what it's doing.

**Tone:**  
Smart · Playful · Slightly Sarcastic · Extremely Competent · Human

---

## 3. Positioning Statement
Better Than Interns helps companies understand their processes, spot automation opportunities, and build AI-powered workflows—through an intelligent, fully automated SaaS that documents and analyzes operational complexity, minus the babysitting.

## 5. Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes
- **Helpful** – Always focused on solving problems.
- **Clever** – Witty observations, smart jokes.
- **Straightforward** – No corporate jargon.
- **Self-aware** – Acknowledges inefficiencies humorously.
- **Human** – Conversational and relatable.

### Examples (Bad vs. Good)

**Bad corporate tone:**  
> “Our platform optimizes operational workflows through strategic AI integration.”

**Better Than Interns tone:**  
> “Your workflows are a mess. We fix them with AI. You’re welcome.”
